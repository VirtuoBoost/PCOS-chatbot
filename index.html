FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

How does the chatbot provide personalized recommendations?

The chatbot analyzes your inputs, including symptoms, lifestyle habits, and health goals, to offer tailored advice on diet, exercise, and treatment options that suit your unique needs.

Can I track my menstrual cycle and symptoms ?

Yes, you can track your menstrual cycle and symptoms with our chatbot. This feature provides personalized insights to help you understand your body and effectively manage your PCOS symptoms.

Is my data safe and confidential ?

Your data is safe and confidential. We prioritize your privacy, employing strict security measures to protect your information and ensure that it remains private and secure at all times.

What types of support does the chatbot offer ?

The chatbot offers support by providing information on PCOS, tracking menstrual cycles, answering health-related questions, suggesting lifestyle changes, and offering personalized recommendations to help manage your symptoms effectively.
